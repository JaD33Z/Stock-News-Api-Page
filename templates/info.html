Usage Info Page



Welcome! With this app you can view your chosen company's percentage difference regarding it's rise/drop in closing price for the last two days. It will also retrieve recent news briefs and links to the articles involving the company of interest. Just enter the ticker symbol and company name in the form on the home page. These symbols are very specific and they must be entered correctly before submitting. For instance, Apple is AAPL, Tesla is TSLA, Microsoft Corporation is MSFT, and so on. Give those examples a try to see the results. There is also a link in the NavBar's Dropdown Menu for a company-name-to-symbol search engine website for reference.
